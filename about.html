The Motus Commitment

Founded 5 years ago, Motus Transportation was created to solve a singular problem: the lack of transparency in modern freight dispatching. We believe that an Owner Operator is a business owner, not just a driver. Our mission is to provide the operational infrastructure, market analysis, and load-matching technology required for you to scale your operation with total confidence.

Operating from our hub in Macedonia, we leverage the unique advantages of a global, 24/7 digital team to provide round-the-clock support for the United States market. This unique business model allows us to focus entirely on your growth, efficiency, and profit margins without the overhead and bureaucracy of massive, stagnant corporations.

The Motus Protocol

Our operational strategy is simple: Standardization. We treat every truck as a unique business entity, designing custom lanes and schedules that align with your specific home-time needs and financial targets.

We don't chase "cheap" freight. We chase long-term, sustainable partnerships. By vetting every load and analyzing every facility, we reduce your downtime and ensure that your revenue per mile stays at the top of the industry standard.
